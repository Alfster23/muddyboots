I am able to offer various dog walks in the Haywards Heath and surrounding area and I am careful to choose locations which are safe but also offer variety and stimulation for your dog.

I walk a maximum of 3 client’s dogs at a time which ensures dogs are well exercised but also have plenty of 1:1 attention. This is safer and less stressful for your pet as large groups of dogs can create a ‘pack’ mentality; not always ideal for your dog or other walkers that we come across. Additionally this means that dogs spend less time being transported, particularly important during hot weather.

As well as small group walks I can also arrange individual walks if required. As much as possible I will arrange walks which are close to your home to minimise the time spent in a vehicle both for the benefit or your dog as well as the environment.

In addition to dog walking I can offer puppy or dog visits as well as cat or small animal feeding.
